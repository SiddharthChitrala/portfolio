Apple Watch Series 8

The Apple Watch Series 8 is a smartwatch that looks like its predecessor, but has some health and safety improvements. It can measure and track body temperature while sleeping, and estimate ovulation date for menstrual cycles. It has a big, brilliant, always-on display that pushes the screen to the edge of the case. It comes in 41mm and 45mm screen sizes, and has 32GB storage, no card slot, and watchOS 9.01.
